HackHers - FAQ

Hello, {{ firstName }}!

We look forward to seeing you at HackHers and hope that you'll have a great time participating in this exciting event. If you have any questions or concerns, please refer to the FAQ below or feel free to contact competitions@hackthehill.com. We'll be more than happy to help you in any way we can. Join our Discord!


Frequently Asked Questions:

When and Where is the Hackathon?
The Hackathon is taking place at CRX, 100 Louis-Pasteur Private, Ottawa, ON K1N 9N3, on February 3rd.

Where will we be doing the hacking?
Throughout the hackathon, feel free to work in one of the many rooms we have allocated to hacking throughout CRX! You may look at the following spreadsheet to see what rooms are available for your hacking!

Will food be provided?
Yes! All hackers will receive free food, snacks, and beverages throughout the event.

How will teams be judged and who will be judging?
Judging will be conducted by a panel of five professionals, ranging from new graduates, to academia professionals and judges from our sponsor, CSE! This is all depending on your chosen track and submissions.

What is the size of teams and what happens if I don't have a team?
If you do not have a team, teams will be formed at a specially dedicated team forming event at the start of the hackathon. Teams contain a maximum of 4 members.

How should I behave at HackHers?
You should be respectful and considerate of the other hackers. Have fun and be cool!

Where can we park?
Don't worry too much as there is plenty of street parking all around Sandy Hill for free. Additionally, paid parking is available at Desmarais and Minto, costing around $9 for the entire day.

What is the schedule for the day?
The schedule can be found here, but is still subject to change.

This is my first hackathon, what should I bring?
Bring your laptop, chargers, and a backpack to hold whatever you might need!

What if I have a question that isn't answered here?
Feel free to contact us on the Discord server or email us at competitions@hackthehill.com.

See you at the opening ceremony at 10:00 am!

The Hack the Hill Team
